Form site for data entry into mongoDB for Coffee Platform.

STYLE GUIDE:
Company Name: "Balzac's Coffee Roasters" Capitalized, and written as the most commonly used form. Abbreviate Company to Co, for instance.
Chain: "YES" If a coffee shop has multiple locations. For example, YES for starbucks, YES for balzacs, NO for manic.
Website: www.balzacs.com Standard URL with www. prefix and tld suffix.

Location: "Liberty Village" The local name of the coffee shop. If not named, nearest major intersection, for example Starbucks "Bathurst/Bloor".
Phone: 4161234567 form. Do not use (), -, ., etc. Will be normalized and formatted with regex before use.
Hours: "Mon-Sat: 7AM-10PM, Sun: 10AM-6PM." Capitalize day of week, dash to denote range, AM/PM included, and comma between days.

Drink: "Americano" Simple drink name, capitalized.
Size: "12oz/16oz"/18oz" Drink sizes in oz. Sizes should be pretty standardized between coffeeshops. Estimations would be fine to a point. "Single/Double" is fine for some drinks.
Price: "2.49/3.26/3.43" Simple decimal form with forward slashes to denote sizes in chronological order.
